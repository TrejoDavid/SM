Graduate Support Program

Through ongoing support during high school, students are able to stay on track and have the support they need to be successful beyond the walls of San Miguel. Because of their hard work coupled with the services provide in the Graduate Support Program- San Miguel graduates are completing high school at rates nearly double that of their neighborhood peers. Graduate support offers the following:

  • Guidance through the High School Application Process
  • High School Visits
  • College Exposure trips
  • Assistance with scholarship applications
  • Guidance through the college application process
  • Alumni retreats
  • Opportunities for community engagement through alumni events, sports, and other community events
  • Tutoring
  • Mentoring
  • Counseling
